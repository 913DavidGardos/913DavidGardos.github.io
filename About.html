Brief History

   My context is mainly artistic, I have studied piano for just under 11 years and since the beginning I was participating in national and international contests of artistic interpretation of particular piano scores.

   However my journey in art took a turn, meaning I lost most of my interest in piano and started to learn guitar on my own, that took about 2 or 3 years to get to a reasonable skill.

   Even though studying music took a significant part of my time, the other part was invested in math and coding. In my senior year in highschool I decided to get into an IT career. Right now I am a student at Politehnica University of Timisoara in my second year.
